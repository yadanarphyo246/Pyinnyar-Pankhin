About the Department

Founded in 1990, our department has been at the forefront of technological education and innovation. We pride ourselves on combining rigorous academic training with practical, real-world applications to prepare students for the dynamic tech industry.

Our mission is to advance the field of computing through cutting-edge research while providing an inclusive environment that fosters creativity, problem-solving, and ethical responsibility in our students.
